EXERCISE GUIDES

Step-by-step with images and AI tips. Click to expand.

Push-Up

Chest · Triceps · Shoulders

Beginner

Step-by-Step Guide

1
High plank — hands slightly wider than shoulders, body straight head to heels.
2
Brace core, squeeze glutes. Hips must not sag or pike.
3
Lower chest toward floor, elbows at 45°. Never flare elbows wide.
4
Push explosively up. Fully extend arms without locking elbows.
5
Inhale down, exhale up. 3 sets × 12–15 reps.
Pectoralis MajorTriceps BrachiiAnterior DeltoidCore
💡 AI Tip: Wrist pain? Try fist push-ups. Beginners start knees on floor.
🦵

Barbell Back Squat

Quads · Glutes · Hamstrings

Intermediate

Step-by-Step Guide

1
Feet shoulder-width, toes out 10–30°. Bar on upper traps.
2
Big breath, brace core (Valsalva). Protects spine under load.
3
Push knees out, sit back and down. Chest tall throughout.
4
Lower until thighs parallel. Knees track over toes.
5
Drive through heels. Squeeze glutes at top. Exhale up.
QuadricepsGluteus MaximusHamstringsErector Spinae
💡 AI Tip: Heels rising? Limited ankle mobility — elevate heels on plates first.
🏋️

Conventional Deadlift

Back · Glutes · Hamstrings

Advanced

Step-by-Step Guide

1
Mid-foot under bar. Feet hip-width. Grip just outside legs.
2
Hinge hips, shins touch bar. Neutral spine — not rounded.
3
Big breath, brace hard, "push the floor away."
4
Bar drags close to body. Hips and shoulders rise equally.
5
Stand tall squeezing glutes. Lower under control.
Erector SpinaeGluteus MaximusHamstringsTrapezius
💡 AI Tip: Never round lower back. Start with Romanian deadlifts to build the pattern.
🧘

Forearm Plank

Core · Glutes · Shoulders

Beginner

Step-by-Step Guide

1
Forearms flat, elbows under shoulders. Clasp hands.
2
Lift hips — straight line head to heels. No sag or pike.
3
Draw belly to spine. Squeeze glutes. Think "rigid plank."
4
Neutral neck, look 12 inches ahead. Don't look up.
5
Breathe slowly. Hold 20–60 sec. Stop when form breaks.
Rectus AbdominisTransverse AbdominisObliquesGlutes
💡 AI Tip: Perfect 30 sec beats sloppy 2 min. Add 5 seconds weekly.
🔝

Pull-Up

Lats · Biceps · Rear Delts

Intermediate

Step-by-Step Guide

1
Hang from bar, overhand grip wider than shoulders. Arms extended.
2
Pack shoulders down and back before pulling.
3
Drive elbows to hips. "Pull bar to chest" not "pull body up."
4
Chin clears bar. Squeeze lats 1 second at top.
5
Lower slowly 2–3 sec. Eccentric builds most strength.
Latissimus DorsiBiceps BrachiiPosterior DeltoidRhomboids
💡 AI Tip: Can't do one? Use bands or practice negative-only pull-ups to build strength.
